❓ How to Play

Movement

Use Arrow Keys to move your ship in all directions.

Your ship fires automatically — just survive and aim!

Waves & Bosses

Each level is a wave of enemy ships. Destroy every enemy to trigger the Boss Fight. Defeat the boss to unlock the next level. Bosses grow stronger with every level.

Power-Ups

  • ⚡ Rapid Fire — doubles your fire rate for 5 seconds
  • 🛡 Shield — absorbs all damage for 8 seconds
  • 🔥 Damage Boost — doubles bullet damage for 8 seconds
  • 🔱 Multi Shot — fires 3 bullets at once for 6 seconds
  • ❤ Health — restores one life (max 5)
  • $ Coin — instant +100 score bonus
  • ☇ Auto-Kill — ship auto-zaps every enemy for 6 seconds

Controls

  • Esc — Pause / Resume
  • R — Restart (on Game Over screen)

Scoring

+10 per regular enemy · +100×level for boss kills · +100 per coin
